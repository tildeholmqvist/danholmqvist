It all begins with an idea.

An art piece should engage, it shall capture interest and it shall touch, create and convey an emotion. Not necessarily an understanding, but it should spark curiosity and trigger interest.

Nestled in a fisherman's village just outside Gothenburg, Sweden, my art studio is a cherished sanctuary where I unleash my creativity.
Here, surrounded by rustic charm, I explore new ideas and techniques while capturing moments of inspiration with my camera.
With over fifty years of professional photography experience, I've cultivated a strong foundation for my artistic endeavors.
Each painting originates from a photograph, transformed through meticulous layering techniques using acrylics and traditional ink.
My artwork is brought to life through careful printing on fine art paper with pigmented inks,
followed by individualized painting.
Each piece is part of a limited edition, marked with its distinct number, reflecting its exclusivity and uniqueness.
