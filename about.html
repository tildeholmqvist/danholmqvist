About Dan Holmqvist

It all begins with an idea.

My art studio is located in an old fisherman cottage outside Gothenburg Sweden, my own place which a truly enjoy. This is where I experiment with ideas and new techniques. This is where I do photography and this is where I create my art. 50 years of professional photography and equally many years of experience has been the preparation for the art I do today. All my paintings have the foundation in something captured by my camera. By working with layers of images I create the foundation for my subsequent paintwork, mainly using acryl and traditional ink. Pictures are printed on fine art paper using pigmented ink and each piece is subsequently painted individually. All artwork are made in a limited edition and are individually numbered.

An art piece should engage, it shall capture interest and it shall touch, create and convey an emotion. Not necessarily an understanding, but it should spark curiosity and trigger interest.
