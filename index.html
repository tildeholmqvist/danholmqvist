Dan Holmqvist

Dan Holmqvist, born in 1953, is a renowned photographer and artist with over 50 years of professional experience. His art is deeply rooted in photography, serving as the foundation for his paintings. Through a unique process involving layering images, primarily using acrylics and traditional ink, Dan creates captivating artworks.
